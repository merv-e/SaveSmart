Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app work?

The app works by helping you set a monthly savings goal and then breaking it down into daily contributions. Each day, the app suggests an amount to save, making it easier to reach your goal.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, your financial data is kept secure. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to ensure your personal and financial information remains safe.

Can I customize my savings goals?

Yes, you can customize your savings goals to fit your needs. The app allows you to set specific amounts, choose different savings durations, and even edit your goals as you go along.
