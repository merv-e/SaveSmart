Tip of the day

Plan your meals: Switching to online grocery shopping means that you can carefully compare the prices of the products you add to your basket, make the most of offers and not be tempted into buying snacks to eat the minute you leave the store! You can also make the most of meal-planning tools like Lollipop to buy only exactly what you need. Explore meal options that are tasty, healthy and kind to your savings. Cook in bulk so you can reduce cooking time and food waste, and freeze leftovers for later.
